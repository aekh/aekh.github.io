    Bio

    Alexander Ek is a Postdoctoral Research in the the Declarative Languages and Artificial Intelligence Section at KU Leuven. In 2022, He received his PhD degree in computer science from Monash University. Most of his research centres around combinatorial optimisation, data- and logic-driven decision-making under uncertainty, proof-logging, and trustworthy and explainable AI/software/elections.
